Journey of a Legend

2008

International Debut

Made ODI debut against Sri Lanka, scoring 12 runs. The beginning of an extraordinary journey.

Virat Kohli made his ODI debut on August 18, 2008, against Sri Lanka in Dambulla. Though he scored only 12 runs, this marked the beginning of one of cricket's greatest careers.

2011

World Cup Victory

Part of the World Cup winning squad, contributing crucial runs in India's historic triumph.

Kohli played a vital role in India's 2011 World Cup campaign, scoring 282 runs. The tournament win on home soil remains one of his most cherished achievements.

Champions Trophy Win

India clinched the ICC Champions Trophy with Kohli's exceptional batting performance.

Kohli was the leading run-scorer in the 2013 Champions Trophy with 296 runs, helping India win the tournament in England.

Led India to historic Test series victories abroad, becoming one of India's greatest Test captains.

Record 50th ODI Century

Broke Sachin Tendulkar's record with his 50th ODI century during World Cup 2023 in India.

On November 15, 2023, against New Zealand in the World Cup semi-final, Kohli scored his record-breaking 50th ODI century, surpassing Sachin's legendary record.
